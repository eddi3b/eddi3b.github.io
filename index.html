About

Edward Balaoro is a seasoned graphic design, entrepreneur, and has a knack for brand management marketing. Edward has worked with computers as a child and gained influence on digital interactive media in the late 90's. His experiences exceled in high school from entering local art competitions to global competitions and still competes when there is an opportunity available. In his early college experience, with his talents, he helped start up several businesses in Downtown San Jose, notably his latest project known as The Glasshouse circa 2013-14. Recently, Edward serves as a committee member and as a design consultant providing promotional marketing material for various events held by San Jose State University's Hospitality Program. Edward is continuing to fine tune his skills and talents whilst completing his studies as a Graphic Design Major in San Jose State University's Design Program.
